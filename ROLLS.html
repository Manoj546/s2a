ROLLS ROYCE UNNATI

Eligibility:

  1. Open for Indian residents only.
  2. Students who have lost their parent(s)/earning family member due to COVID-19 pandemic.
  3. Applicants must be pursuing graduation (general & professional) courses in STEM subjects.
  4. Applicants should be currently enrolled and continuing their education.
  5. Annual family income of the applicant should be less than INR 6,00,000 (6 Lakh) from all sources.
Benefits:
Each selected scholar will receive a one-time fixed scholarship of INR 25,000*.
*The scholarship amount can be utilized only for academic expenses which includes tuition fees, hostel fees (on-campus only), internet, laptop, books, stationery, online learning, etc.
Documents:
The following documents need to be uploaded along the online scholarship application:

  • Passport size photograph
  • Aadhaar card
  • Family income proof (Salary Slip, Form 16, Income Certificate, ITR, etc.)
  • Marksheet of previous educational qualification
  • Current academic year admission proof (fee receipt/admission letter/institution identity card/bonafide certificate)
  • Proof of crisis (death certificate of the lost earning member)
  • Reference from a person who knows the familyâ€™s crisis (Can be a school teacher, doctor, head of school, college, or a government officer, etc.)
  • Bank account details of the scholarship applicant or parents (cancelled cheque/passbook copy)
