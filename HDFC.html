HDFC Bank Parivartan's COVID Crisis Support Scholarship Program 2021

Eligibility:

  1. Open for Indian nationals only.
  2. The students must be currently studying in Class 1 to 12.
  3. Students who have been through any of the below two crisis situations–
    • Students who lost either or both of their parent(s)/earning member(s) since January 2020
    • Students whose earning family member lost their employment (or livelihood) during the pandemic
  4. Annual family income of the applicant should be less than INR 6,00,000 (6 Lakh) .
  5. Children of employees of HDFC Bank and Buddy4Study are not eligible.
Benefits:
For students of Class 1 to 5 – INR 15,000
For students of Class 6 to 8 – INR 18,000
For students of Class 9 to 12 – INR 21,000
NOTE: Scholarship fund can be utilized only for academic expense purposes which includes tuition fees, hostel fees, food, internet, online learning device, books, stationery, etc.
Documents:
The following documents need to be uploaded along the online scholarship application:

  • Marksheet of the previous educational degree (2019-20) (NOTE: In case, you don’t have a marksheet for 2019-20 session, please upload the marksheet for 2018-19 session.)
  • A government-issued identity proof (Aadhaar card/voter identity card/driving license/PAN card)
  • Current year admission proof (fee receipt/admission letter/institution identity card/bonafide certificate) (2020-21)
  • Crisis document (Death certificate of parent(s) or proof of job loss)
  • Reference from 2 persons who know the family’s crisis (Can be a school teacher, doctor, head of school, college, or a government officer, etc.)
  • Bank account details of the scholarship applicant
  • Photograph of the applicant
